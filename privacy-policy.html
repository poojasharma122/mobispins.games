Privacy Policy

At Mobispins.games your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and games.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Mobispins, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information – such as your username, email address, or social login details.
  • Gameplay Data – including game progress, scores, and in-game purchases.
  • Device & Technical Data – such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and cookies.
  • Optional Information – if you contact us directly, we may keep a record of your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Mobispins gaming experience.
  • Save your progress, rewards, and account history.
  • Send you updates, bonuses, or promotional offers (if you opt in).
  • Maintain site security and prevent fraud.

3. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, improve gameplay, and analyze site performance
  • You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but some features may not function properly.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or trade your personal information.
  • We may share data with trusted partners (e.g., hosting providers, analytics services) who help us operate Mobispins.
  • If required by law, we may share information with legal authorities.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security of your data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Mobispins is intended for players 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover a child’s data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of promotional communications.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of Mobispins means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us
